Hello there!

Founded by Shay, a passionate maker dedicated to slowing down fashion, we create crochet garments and accessories that are timeless, sustainable, and lovingly handcrafted. By choosing Croshay’s, you’re not only purchasing a garment — you’re supporting ethical craftsmanship and saying no to the waste of fast fashion.
